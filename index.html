John Brady

John is a Visual / User Interface Designer based in Austin, Texas. He fell in love with digital design nearly 20 years ago after discovering the power of Adobe Photoshop, and has been working to refine his design skills ever since.

He enjoys fully engaging his creative and analytical sides in ways for him to find optimal and polished solutions for digital platforms. His favorite thing about the design process is telling a story with every aspect of the brand’s identity -- all the way down to subtle and intricate details that may go unnoticed, but subconsciously help reinforce the brand.
